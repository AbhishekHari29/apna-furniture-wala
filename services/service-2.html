Furniture Repair

Your furniture and wood surfaces can become damaged over time or eventually going to show signs of wear and tear. Instead of throwing away your worn-out furniture, recycle the piece with repair services from us.

Each of our technicians is equipped to provide immediate on-site, in-store, and home furniture repairs in fabric, upholstery, leather, vinyl, wood, and machinery. Our store is strategically located in Royapettah, Chennai and our team is available 24/7 to help us complete both small and large projects in the shortest possible time.

We offer a variety of residential and commercial repair and restoration services to meet your needs and you will definitely receive high-quality services and fantastic restored furniture. Along with repair and restoration services, the workshop is engaged in the manufacture of custom-made furniture.

Whenever our team refurbishes new home furniture, we invest time, care and effort into the renovation and refurbishment. Our unique home furnishing and refinishing process allows us to apply our incredible creativity to an incredible range of furniture types.

Repair Services:

  1. Furniture Repair & Restore: Return the original look and beauty to furniture that has been damaged or lost its functionality.
  2. Furniture Painting: Painting wood furniture gives life to new and old pieces. Our process includes carefully sanding and repairing blemishes, followed by applying primer and paint.
  3. Dent / Gouge / Scratch Repair: We use special products and techniques to fill in dents, gouges, and scratches to restore your furniture.
  4. Expert Colour Matching: We can custom colour-match wood furnishings, millwork and cabinetry to existing furniture colours and stains.
